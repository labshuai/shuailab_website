Xinru Su, is a Class of 2024 master's student specializing in Ophthalmology, is currently studying at the Research Center of the Second Hospital of Dalian Medical University. Her research focuses on the role and mechanisms of the ubiquitin – proteasome system in retinal diseases. She has been awarded the First Prize in the Campus Selection of the China International College Students Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, received the Excellent Award in the university-level “Challenge Cup” competition, and has been granted an Academic Scholarship.
