Pengfei Zhang, Ph.D., is a Professor in the School of Optoelectronic Engineering and Instrumentation Science at Dalian University of Technology. His research specializes in advanced biomedical optical imaging and instrumentation, with a focus on Optical Coherence Tomography, Scanning Laser Ophthalmoscopy, and Adaptive Optics. His work aims to achieve cellular-resolution in vivo imaging, primarily for groundbreaking research in retinal and ocular science.
