Zixuan Meng, a Class of 2025 professional master's degree candidate in Ophthalmology under the supervision of Professor Shuai Wang. , is undergoing clinical and research training at the Second Affiliated Hospital of Dalian Medical University. Currently, she is engaged in research under the mentorship of Professor Wang Shuai. Her research focuses on the application of AI models in lacrimal metabolomics for dry eye syndrome, aiming to provide scientific evidence for early disease diagnosis and the development of personalized treatment plans. She was awarded the third prize at the provincial level in the Liaoning Provincial Residency Training Speech Contest.
