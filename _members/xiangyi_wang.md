Xiangyi Wang , a 2024 ophthalmology graduate student in Professor Shuai Wang's research lab, is undergoing clinical and research training at the Second Affiliated Hospital of Dalian Medical University. At present, under the guidance from Dr.Wang , she is dedicated to integrating clinical practice with scientific research, exploring the use of artificial intelligence models for antimicrobial peptide screening from ocular surface. Her goal is to contribute to advancements in ophthalmic diagnosis and treatment within real clinical settings.She is looking forward to continuous growth within the team and advancing alongside laboratory colleagues.
