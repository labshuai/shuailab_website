Liu Chang, born in Henan in 1999, was admitted to the Department of Otolaryngology of the Second Affiliated Hospital of Dalian Medical University in 2024 to study for a master's degree. Her main research interests are the molecular mechanism of chronic sinusitis and the effects of adenoid and tonsil hypertrophy on children's facial development.
